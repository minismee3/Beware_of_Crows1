We are
Beware of Crows

Based in the West End of Glasgow, we are a small business specialising in custom designed and handpainted skateboard decks.
With an array of sizes, colours and graphics, we offer a completely personalised and unique service. You can buy one of our boards or you can send us your board for a re-vamp.

Our Boards

The Process

Hand-picked boards are stripped and sanded down to be primed and painted on with four coats of white. Custom designs are then drawn and hand-painted with bold acryllic paints and finished off with thin coats of either satin or gloss laquer for protection and to complete the look.
